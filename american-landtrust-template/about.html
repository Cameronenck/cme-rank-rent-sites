The Founder

Why I Started American Land Trust

I'm Demetri Suliman — a proud Texan and a family man. I built this company to serve the people who own a piece of this country.

It started when a family friend inherited rural acreage she couldn't afford to keep. The "investors" who contacted her sent lowball postcards and pressure tactics. The agents wanted a year and a 10% commission. Nobody would simply tell her what the land was worth and why.

"Landowners don't need another slick buyer. They need a straight answer, a fair number, and a closing they can trust. That's the whole business."

Today we've helped hundreds of families across 31 states turn land they no longer need into money they can use — every deal in writing, every closing through a licensed title company, every seller treated the way I'd want my own family treated.

Demetri Suliman Founder, American Land Trust
