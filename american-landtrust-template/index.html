Full Transparency

How We Calculate Your Offer

Most land buyers won't show you their math. We build every offer from four factors — and we walk you through each one.

Comparable Sales

Recent sold prices for similar parcels in your county — not tax value, not guesswork.

Access & Road Frontage

Legal access, road frontage, and easements are the biggest drivers of land value.

Utilities, Terrain & Zoning

Power at the road, floodplain, slope, and county zoning all factor into the number.

Holding Time & Demand

How long land sits on the market in your area — we take on that risk so you don't have to.

Every written offer includes the comparable sales we used — ask us anything.
