What We Do

Lancaster County Tree Services

Full-service tree care from removal to trimming to stump grinding. Licensed, insured, and ready to work.

Tree Removal

Complete tree removal for trees of any size — dead, diseased, hazardous, or just in the way. We drop, section, and haul everything away clean.

Tree Trimming

Precision crown trimming and canopy thinning to keep your trees healthy, shaped, and safe. We remove dead branches and improve structure.

Stump Grinding

Get rid of ugly stumps for good. Our grinder takes stumps down 6–12 inches below grade so you can replant, sod, or pave over the spot.

Emergency Tree Service

Storm damage, fallen trees on houses, blocked driveways — we respond 24/7. Fast mobilization throughout Lancaster County.

Lot Clearing

Clearing land for a new build, addition, or driveway? We clear trees, brush, and stumps efficiently — leaving the lot clean and ready.

Tree Health Assessment

Not sure if that tree needs to come down? Our arborists evaluate disease, root damage, lean, and structural integrity — so you get honest advice.
