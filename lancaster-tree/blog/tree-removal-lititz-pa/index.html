Tree Removal in Lititz PA — Local Service, Honest Prices

We've been working trees in Lancaster County for going on 16 years now. Why Lititz Has More Tree Issues Than You'd Think

Lititz is a beautiful borough — the historic downtown, the older neighborhoods off Broad Street, the farms on the edges of town. But that maturity means a lot of the trees on residential properties are 40, 50, 60 years old. That's when things start going wrong.

We see a lot of emerald ash borer damage in and around Lititz. That pest has been tearing through Pennsylvania's ash tree population for years and if you have ash trees on your property that haven't been treated, there's a good chance they're already in trouble. A dead or dying ash doesn't come down on its own schedule — a wind event or a heavy wet snow decides that for you.

We also see a lot of silver maples and Bradford pears that were planted decades ago and are now splitting under their own weight. Bradford pears in particular are notorious for poor branch structure — they look fine right up until a storm takes half the tree off and puts it through a fence or a car. If you have one and it's getting big, it's worth having someone look at it.

What Tree Removal Costs in Lititz PA

I'll give you real numbers instead of the runaround. Most residential tree removals in Lititz run somewhere between $400 and $1,800. Here's what moves the price:

  • Size: A 15-foot ornamental or ornamental flowering tree is straightforward. A 70-foot oak with a 24-inch trunk is a different day's work entirely.
  • Location: Open yard access versus a tree wedged between two structures — that determines how much rigging is involved. More rigging means more time and more crew.
  • Deadwood: A tree that's already dead is often more unpredictable to work than a healthy one. Dead wood is brittle and doesn't bend — it breaks. That takes more caution and more time.
  • Stump grinding: We quote this separately. It's usually $100–$250 additional and it's much cheaper to do it the same day we're already there.

We give free written estimates before any work starts. No surprises when the invoice comes.

What to Expect When We Come Out

When we show up to quote your job in Lititz, we walk the property with you and look at the tree from every angle — root zone, canopy, lean direction, what's in the fall zone. We talk through the options. Sometimes a tree that looks bad from the street is actually in better shape than it appears. Sometimes it's worse. Either way, we'll be straight with you.

On the day of removal, our crew shows up with the right equipment for the job — we're not improvising with the wrong tools. We section the tree down, manage where the wood falls, and clean up when we're done. Chips and brush leave with us. If you want the firewood rounds, we'll stack them. If you want everything gone, we take it.

We're fully insured — general liability and workers' comp — and we'll hand you the certificates before we start. If any tree company won't do that, find a different one.
