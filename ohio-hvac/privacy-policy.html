Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

When you use our contact forms or request a cash offer, we collect:

  • Your name and contact information (phone number, email address)

We use the information you provide to:

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages and calls from American Land Trust regarding your property inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. We may use automated calling systems. You can opt out at any time by:

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
