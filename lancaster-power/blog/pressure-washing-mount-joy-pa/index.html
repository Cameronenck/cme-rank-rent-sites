House Washing in Mount Joy

The most common call we get in Mount Joy is for a house wash. Vinyl siding is everywhere in the newer neighborhoods, and it builds up algae streaks, oxidation, and mildew faster than most homeowners expect — by the time you notice green or gray streaking running down from the roofline or collecting on the north and west-facing walls, the growth has already been working its way in for a while.

We soft wash all vinyl and painted wood siding. That means low pressure and a biodegradable cleaning solution that kills the organism at the root level, not just scrubs it off the surface temporarily. The difference between soft washing and blasting siding with high pressure is significant — high pressure forces water behind panels, cracks caulk around windows and trim, and can strip paint from wood. Soft wash doesn't create those problems and produces a cleaner, longer-lasting result.

For the older homes in the Mount Joy borough proper — brick fronts, painted stone, older wood siding — we back the pressure down further and use solutions appropriate for those surfaces. Older brick and mortar needs a light hand. We've seen the damage that high-pressure washing does to historic and older construction in Lancaster County, and there's no reason to risk it when lower pressure and the right chemistry does a better job anyway.

Driveway and Concrete Cleaning in Mount Joy

Concrete driveways in central Pennsylvania take a beating from the freeze-thaw cycle every winter. That repeated expansion and contraction opens up the surface over time, making it progressively easier for oil, tire marks, and algae to penetrate. Standard pressure washing removes surface staining, but oil and grease require pre-treatment with a degreaser before washing to actually pull the stain out rather than just dilute it.

We use a commercial rotary surface cleaner on flat concrete — it covers a wide, consistent path at controlled pressure, which eliminates the stripe marks you get from sweeping a wand back and forth. Oil spots get called out and pre-treated before we start so there are no surprises on the final price.

Concrete patios, front walkways, and sidewalks are easy add-ons to a driveway job. Equipment is already set up and on-site, so the incremental cost is low compared to booking them as a separate trip.

Roof Soft Washing in Mount Joy

If you've noticed dark streaking on your roof in Mount Joy — those gray-black stains that spread from the ridgeline down across the shingles — that's Gloeocapsa magma, a cyanobacterium that feeds on the limestone filler in asphalt shingles. It's everywhere in Lancaster County. Pressure washing removes the visual staining but doesn't kill the organism, damages the shingle surface, and voids most manufacturer warranties in the process. The streaks come back the next season.

Soft washing is the manufacturer-recommended method: low pressure, specialized chemical solution applied to the roof that kills the algae at the cell level. The dead growth rinses off over the next few weeks of rain. Done correctly, results last three to five years. It's a straightforward job when the right chemistry is used, and we've done it on a wide variety of roof pitches and shingle types across Lancaster County.
