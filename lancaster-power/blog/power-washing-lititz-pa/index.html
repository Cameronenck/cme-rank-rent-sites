Power Washing Lititz PA — Local Service, Honest Prices

Published May 2026 • 6 min read • By Lancaster Power Washing Pros

Lititz is one of my favorite towns to work in. The historic streetscape, the older homes with wood siding and brick facades, the tree canopy over half the neighborhoods — it's a great place to live, but all that character comes with a cleaning bill. Algae grows fast on north-facing rooflines. Organic staining from trees builds up on driveways season after season. And a lot of the older siding in the borough needs a careful touch that high-pressure machines can't provide.

We do a lot of work in Lititz and the surrounding Warwick Township area. Here's what we typically see, what we do about it, and what you can expect when you call us for a quote.

House Washing in Lititz

The biggest thing I see on Lititz homes is green and black biological staining — algae, mildew, and mold — on siding, particularly on north-facing walls and anywhere with tree shade. This stuff isn't just cosmetic. Left alone, it retains moisture against the surface and slowly degrades paint, wood, and even vinyl over time.

We soft wash siding in Lititz rather than blasting it with high pressure. Soft washing uses a biodegradable cleaning solution at low pressure to kill the biological growth at the root level, then rinses clean. The results last longer than a pressure wash because you're actually killing the organism rather than just scraping the surface. For older homes with original wood trim or delicate caulk lines, soft washing is also safer — high pressure can force water behind panels and cause more damage than the staining you were trying to fix.

A typical Lititz house wash runs $150–$350 depending on size, and it takes 2–4 hours. We don't need you to be home — we handle all the setup and cleanup ourselves.

Driveway and Concrete Cleaning

Lancaster County winters are rough on driveways. Road salt, freeze-thaw cycles, and tree debris all take a toll. By the time spring rolls around, most Lititz driveways have significant dark staining, oil spots near the garage, and biological growth in the shaded sections.

Pressure washing concrete is straightforward, but the technique matters. You want consistent overlap and the right tip angle to avoid striping — that zebra-stripe pattern you see on driveways that were washed unevenly. We use surface cleaner attachments on concrete for even results every time.

If your concrete is porous and stained deep, we can apply a cleaning pre-treatment to loosen embedded grime before the pressure wash. After cleaning, we can also apply a concrete sealer to protect the surface and make future cleaning faster. Driveway cleaning in Lititz typically runs $80–$200 depending on square footage.

Roof Soft Washing

Black streaks on your roof are not dirt — they're algae, specifically Gloeocapsa magma. This organism spreads from spores, feeds on the limestone filler in asphalt shingles, and works down the roof face from the ridge. The north and west faces of Lititz rooftops tend to show it earliest because they get less direct sun and stay damp longer.

Pressure washing a roof strips granules off the shingles and voids most manufacturer warranties. The correct method is soft washing: a low-pressure application of a biocide solution that kills the algae, followed by a rinse. The staining fades over a few weeks as the dead organisms wash away in the rain. Results typically last 3–5 years.

Roof cleaning in Lititz runs $300–$600 for a typical single-family home. We're fully insured and carry appropriate coverage for roof work — ask us for proof of insurance before you hire anyone for this.

Deck and Patio Cleaning

Wood decks in Lititz see significant weathering — gray discoloration, black mildew staining in the gaps between boards, and green algae on the surface. Before you re-stain or seal a deck, it has to be clean. Stain applied over a dirty deck peels within a season.

We wash decks with a wood-safe detergent at controlled pressure. For decks that have never been cleaned or sealed, we may apply a wood brightener after washing to bring back the natural tone before you stain. Deck washing runs $100–$300 depending on size and condition. If you want us to coordinate with your staining contractor or handle the full prep, we can do that too.
