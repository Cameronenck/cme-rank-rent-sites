What Drives Construction Debris Pricing in Peoria

Not all construction waste is the same. Pricing depends on three main factors: volume, material weight, and how long the haul takes. Most debris removal is priced by the fraction of the truck your load fills, but heavy materials like concrete, brick, and tile get priced by weight too because disposal costs more at the transfer station.

  • Volume of material. A single-room demo — pulling old carpet, baseboards, and drywall from one bedroom — fills roughly a quarter truck. A full bathroom gut-out or kitchen demo can fill half a truck or more. The bigger the load, the higher the price.
  • Material type and weight. Lightweight materials like drywall, insulation, and lumber are cheap to haul. Heavy materials — concrete blocks, brick, roofing shingles, ceramic tile — cost more per cubic yard because they weigh more and some require special disposal handling.
  • Separation vs. mixed debris. Mixed loads (everything thrown together) are fine and that's how most residential jobs work. Contractors who separate materials — wood from concrete, metal from drywall — sometimes qualify for lower rates because sorted materials are easier to recycle.
  • Access and loading. Ground-level access with a clear path to the truck is the simplest situation. Basement debris that has to be carried up stairs, or attic insulation that needs to be bagged, adds labor time and is reflected in the quote.
  • Job site vs. home cleanup. Active contractor job sites that need regular scheduled pickups get better pricing through volume agreements. One-time residential cleanups are quoted by the load.

DIY Dumpster Rental vs. Hiring a Crew in Peoria

The dumpster rental math often looks cheaper on paper. A standard 10-yard dumpster in Peoria runs about $300 to $400 for a week. But you also need to load it yourself, stay within weight limits (overages cost extra), and schedule pickup at the end. For most residential renovation cleanups, a full-service junk removal crew ends up being comparable in price and significantly less work on your end.

The main advantage of a dumpster rental is timing flexibility — it sits there while you work and you fill it on your schedule.
