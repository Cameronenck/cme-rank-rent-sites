What Commercial Junk Removal Actually Covers

When we talk commercial cleanout, here's what we handle:

  • Office furniture and fixtures — desks, chairs, filing cabinets, conference tables, shelving, cubicle dividers, broken office equipment
  • Retail equipment and displays — old shelving, display fixtures, mannequins, broken point-of-sale systems, signage
  • Warehouse and storage — pallets, packaging material, broken shelving units, machinery parts, old stock
  • Construction and renovation debris — drywall, flooring scraps, tile, carpet, lumber, roofing materials, old insulation
  • Commercial appliances — industrial refrigerators, stoves, washing machines, broken HVAC units
  • Electronics and IT equipment — old computers, servers, monitors, printers, cables (proper e-waste disposal included)
  • Miscellaneous commercial junk — broken machinery, old signage, non-hazardous waste, anything cluttering the space

The rule is simple: if it's in the way, taking up space, and you need it gone, we haul it. We don't take hazardous materials — paint, chemicals, refrigerants, asbestos, or anything requiring special handling. Tell us what you're dealing with and we'll let you know straight whether we can take it.

Office Furniture Disposal and Donation

A lot of office furniture leaving a business is still good — chairs, desks, cabinets that just aren't being used anymore. Rather than have all of it end up in a landfill, we route usable pieces to local donation organizations. It keeps good furniture in use and helps out places that need it.

We'll ask before we haul. If you've got items that can be donated, we handle the logistics. If it needs to go to the landfill, we take it there. Either way, your space gets cleared and you're not stuck managing the details.

Renovation and Construction Debris Cleanup

If your business is renovating — new flooring, walls coming down, HVAC system being replaced — we handle the debris cleanup. Drywall, old flooring, lumber scraps, tile, roofing material, insulation — we load it all up and dispose of it properly.

The beauty of using us for renovation cleanup is timing. We can schedule the haul for right after the work's done, so your contractor doesn't have to deal with storage or multiple trips to the dump. We show up, load everything, and your space is ready for the next phase of the project.
