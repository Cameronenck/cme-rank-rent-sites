What Junk Removal Costs in Washington IL

We price based on how much space your load takes in the truck — not by the item, not by weight. You get a firm quote after we take a look. No pricing over the phone based on guesses, no surprise charges after the job. Here's a general range:

  • Small load (1–3 items, roughly 1/8 truckload): $100–$150
  • Medium load (a few rooms' worth of furniture and boxes): $200–$350
  • Large load (garage cleanout, partial basement): $350–$500
  • Full truckload (major cleanout, full estate, whole basement): $500–$650+

Heavy items like appliances, safes, or fitness equipment take more labor per cubic foot, and we build that into the quote upfront. Washington is a regular stop for us — no extra mileage fee, no minimum bumped up because you're outside Peoria city limits.
