Mattress Removal Cost in Peoria IL — What You'll Actually Pay

Published May 23, 2026  •  Peoria Junk Removal Pros  •  5 min read

Getting rid of an old mattress in Peoria should be simple, but most people have no idea what it actually costs until they call around. Prices vary more than you might expect, and a few key factors can push your total up or keep it low. This guide breaks down exactly what you can expect to pay for mattress removal in Peoria, IL in 2026.

What Mattress Removal Costs in Peoria IL

For a single mattress pickup in Peoria, you are looking at a range of $60 to $120 on average. That price covers the labor to haul the mattress out of your home, load it onto the truck, and handle responsible disposal or donation drop-off. If you have a box spring, that typically adds $20 to $40 to the total.

King-size mattresses, multiple mattresses, or jobs that require crew members to navigate stairs or tight hallways will land closer to the upper end of that range. The table below gives you a realistic look at what different scenarios cost locally.

Item / Scenario Typical Size Estimated Cost
Twin or full mattress (single) Standard $60 – $85
Queen mattress (single) Standard $75 – $100

Mattress + box spring set Any size $100 – $150

These figures reflect local Peoria market conditions. Prices from national chain junk haulers often run 20 to 30 percent higher for the same work, which is one reason many Peoria homeowners prefer calling a local operator.

What Drives the Price Up or Down

Understanding what affects the final number helps you get an accurate quote and avoid surprises on the day of pickup.

Size and Weight

A twin mattress weighs 40 to 50 pounds and loads quickly. A king-size memory foam mattress can weigh over 130 pounds and requires two crew members to maneuver safely. Heavier, bulkier mattresses cost more because they take more labor and more truck space.

Quantity

The first mattress is always the most expensive per-unit because it triggers the base service fee. A second or third mattress added to the same job costs significantly less on a per-piece basis. If you are clearing out a spare room, a rental property, or multiple bedrooms, schedule everything together.

Access and Location Inside the Home

Ground-floor removal from a room adjacent to an exterior door is the easiest and cheapest scenario. Upper floors, basements, narrow staircases, or tight hallways all require extra time and crew effort, which gets priced in. Be upfront about access when you call so your quote is accurate.

Condition and Special Circumstances

Standard-condition mattresses move through the system quickly. Mattresses with visible mold, significant water damage, or suspected pest infestation are a different story. Many haulers add a surcharge for these, and some decline them entirely. Always disclose condition when booking.

Bundling Saves You Money

If you are also dealing with a bed frame, dresser, or other bedroom furniture, bundling everything into one job is almost always cheaper than scheduling separate pickups. A mattress added onto a furniture removal job in Peoria typically costs $40 to $70 less than a standalone mattress trip. The truck is already there, the crew is already there, and the incremental cost of adding one more item is low.

Similarly, if you are in the middle of a larger cleanout, check out our breakdown of general junk removal costs in Peoria to see how bundling multiple categories affects your overall price.

Curbside vs. Full-Service Pickup

Some haulers offer a lower rate if you drag the mattress to the curb yourself. This can save you $15 to $25, but it only makes sense if you are physically able to do it and the mattress is not on an upper floor. Full-service removal means the crew comes inside, handles all the lifting, and clears the mattress from wherever it sits. For most homeowners, the convenience is worth the small price difference.

Where Does the Mattress Go?

Responsible haulers in Peoria do not simply dump mattresses at the landfill. A mattress in usable condition gets donated to a local organization or charity reseller. Mattresses that are past their useful life get broken down for material recycling when possible: foam, springs, and fabric can all be separated and processed. When you call Peoria Junk Removal Pros, we handle disposal the right way so you do not have to think about it.

How to Get an Accurate Quote

Getting a quote is straightforward. When you call, have the following information ready:

  • Number and sizes of mattresses (twin, full, queen, king)
  • Whether box springs are included
  • Floor level and any access challenges (stairs, narrow hallways)
  • Condition of the mattress (standard, stained, water-damaged, or pest-related)
  • Whether you have other items to remove at the same time

With that information, we can give you a firm price before we show up. No surprise charges at the door.
