What Affects the Price of a Garage Cleanout

Size of the space. A single-car garage takes less time and fewer trips than a double-car garage stuffed to the ceiling. Square footage matters, but so does how tightly everything is packed.

Type and weight of items. A garage with light boxes and plastic storage bins costs less than one filled with old car parts, concrete blocks, or metal filing cabinets. Heavy items require more labor and sometimes a second trip.

How much you're keeping. If you're selling items on the side or donating certain things, that sorting takes time. We can coordinate donations to local Peoria charities or help you list items for sale, but it extends the job. Pure haul-away jobs move faster.

Accessibility and location. A garage on ground level with easy access is straightforward. If your garage is on a slope, uphill, or blocked by a parked car, it adds time. We'll work around it, but let us know upfront.

Hazardous materials. If the garage contains old paint, batteries, oil, or other hazmat, disposal costs more. Most homeowners don't know what's hazmat—we'll identify it and let you know the additional cost upfront.

How Can I Save Money on a Garage Cleanout?

Sort items yourself ahead of time. Spend a weekend going through everything and marking what's junk, what's trash, and what you might sell or donate. When we arrive, we just load and haul. This saves time and cost—usually cuts 20-30% off the total.

Sell items before we come. Host a garage sale a week or two before scheduling us. Remove items you can get money for, and we haul the leftovers. You keep the sale proceeds and pay less for junk removal.

Donate instead of disposing. We work with Peoria-area charities (Goodwill, Salvation Army, habitat for Humanity ReStore) to donate furniture and decent items. It's often the same price as disposal, but you get the tax write-off.

Bundle with other junk removal jobs. Cleaning out the garage plus clearing the yard, basement, or house? One job, one trip, one fee. Bundling saves you 10-15% per job.

Schedule mid-week, mid-morning. Tuesday–Thursday, 9 AM–2 PM is our slowest window. We sometimes offer same-week discounts for off-peak scheduling.

What Happens to the Stuff We Remove?

Nothing goes straight to a landfill if it can be salvaged or recycled. You're helping the environment and keeping hazardous material out of the waste stream.
