What Makes Furniture Removal More Expensive

Stairs and tight spaces. If we're pulling a couch down a narrow staircase or out of a finished basement, that takes longer and needs careful maneuvering. It's not a deal-breaker, but it affects the time. We'll quote it accurately.

Condition of the furniture. A couch in good condition we can donate at no extra charge—you get the tax write-off and we avoid the landfill fee. Damaged, stained, or broken furniture gets hauled and disposed of normally. No surprises there.

Distance to truck. If you live on a long driveway or the furniture is in a detached garage, it takes more labor to move it all the way to the truck. It's built into our pricing, but it matters.

Volume of pieces. Removing one couch is one trip. Removing a full house of furniture might need two truck runs, which adds time and fuel. We quote based on the total volume.

Common Questions About Furniture Removal

Can you remove furniture from upstairs?

Yes. We navigate stairs, tight hallways, and doorways. We use dollies and protective pads to prevent damage. Upstairs furniture costs the same as ground-floor—we factor in the extra work. Just tell us where it is during your estimate.

What if my furniture doesn't fit through the front door?

We've handled this hundreds of times. Couches, beds, and dressers get disassembled if needed. If something truly won't come out through any opening, we move it to a safe location in your home and give you honest options. It's rare and usually not expensive to solve.

Do you remove mattresses?

Yes. Mattresses are a common removal—from estates, old apartments, or just upgrades. We remove them, dispose of them properly, and it's included in our standard pricing. Queen or king mattress removal runs $100-$150.

Absolutely. If you want to list pieces on Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist, go for it. We'll work around your sales timeline. Once pieces don't sell, we can haul the rest. We're flexible.

What if I change my mind after you quote me?

No problem. The quote is good for 30 days. If you want to add or remove items, we'll adjust the price and reschedule. We're not going to pressure you into anything.

Do you haul away office furniture and cubicles?

Yes. Commercial furniture removal—desks, filing cabinets, cubicle panels, chairs—is part of our service. Call us for a quote. Office cleanouts often qualify for volume discounts.
