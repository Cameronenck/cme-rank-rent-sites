Bulk Trash Removal Cost in Peoria IL — 2026 Pricing Guide

Updated June 9, 2026 • 8 min read • By Peoria Junk Removal Pros

Most households generate more junk than they expect. Spring cleaning, downsizing, moving, or just clearing out a garage or basement can pile up fast. The city of Peoria's standard trash service only picks up what fits in your weekly bin. Anything bigger than that — old furniture, broken appliances, electronics, cardboard, construction scraps — is your problem until it's gone. That's where bulk trash removal comes in.

Bulk trash removal in Peoria typically runs $150 to $500+ depending on the volume and type of material. Small loads of mixed junk are at the low end. Full truckloads of furniture, appliances, and construction debris hit the high end. Here's what determines your final cost.

What Counts as Bulk Trash

Bulk trash is any collection of mixed items that do not fit in your weekly trash cans or require special handling. The most common jobs we handle in Peoria:

  • Old furniture — couches, chairs, tables, dressers, bed frames
  • Broken or outdated appliances — refrigerators, washers, dryers, ovens, dishwashers
  • Electronics — old TVs, monitors, computer equipment, audio equipment
  • Cardboard boxes, packing materials, and moving debris
  • General household clutter, stacks of items, and miscellaneous junk
  • Construction scraps — drywall, wood, flooring, roofing materials
  • Yard debris mixed with household items in one load

What we cannot haul: hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, oil, refrigerants, asbestos, propane tanks, or other regulated waste. If you are unsure whether something is hazardous, call and describe it — we can advise.

How We Price Bulk Trash Jobs

We price by volume — the fraction of the truck your junk fills. We assess the load before we start hauling, walk you through the estimate, and give you a flat rate with no surprises. Most residential bulk trash jobs fall into the 1/4 to 1/2 truck range.

One factor people overlook: weight. A couch and a refrigerator take up similar space but the refrigerator weighs significantly more. If your load is unusually dense or heavy, that is factored into the quote. Call us before you load everything up so we can give you an accurate picture.

For larger projects like full estate cleanouts or post-renovation bulk removal, we can give you a rough estimate based on photos sent by text. Call 309-861-9815 and we can discuss options.

Bulk Trash vs. Dumpster Rental in Peoria

Dumpster rentals work well for sustained projects where you are loading debris over days or weeks. You pay a delivery fee, a rental period fee, and a disposal fee. Total cost for a dumpster typically runs $400–$600+ for a week or two. The advantage: unlimited time to load on your schedule.

Bulk trash removal works better for one-time cleanouts. We show up, haul everything in one trip, and it is gone the same day. No dumpster sitting in your driveway for a week. No heavy lifting on your part. For a single large cleanup, bulk removal is often cheaper and faster. For an ongoing renovation, a dumpster may make more sense.

Not sure which fits your situation? Call us and we can advise. Sometimes a combination works best — dumpster for debris, bulk removal for appliances and furniture.
