Common Questions

Questions About Selling an Inherited Property

Do I need to wait for probate to close?

+
Not always. In many cases we can begin the process and structure the deal around your probate timeline. We work with estate attorneys regularly and know how to move efficiently. Contact us and we will assess your specific situation.

What if multiple heirs are involved?

+
We handle multi-heir sales all the time. All parties need to agree to the sale, but we make the process straightforward. We will communicate clearly with everyone involved and ensure the paperwork is clean.

Do I need to clean out the property?

+
No. We buy inherited homes exactly as they are — furniture, belongings, and all. Take what you want and leave the rest. We handle the rest after closing.

How fast can you close?

+
Once all heirs agree and any probate requirements are satisfied, we can close in as few as 7-14 days. We move at your pace — fast when you need it, patient when you don't.
