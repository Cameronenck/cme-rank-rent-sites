Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

We start with a quick call or form submission to learn about your property. Within 24 hours, we will reach out with a fair cash offer. If you accept, you pick the closing date and we handle all the paperwork. Most closings happen in 7-14 days.

Will I get a fair price for my home?

Yes. Our offers are based on real local market data, recent comparable sales in your area, and the current condition of your property — not lowball algorithms designed to take advantage of motivated sellers.

Do I need to make repairs or clean the house?

Never. We buy homes completely as-is. Leave whatever you do not want behind — furniture, junk, belongings. We handle everything after closing.

Are there really no fees or commissions?

Zero. No agent commissions, no closing costs charged to you, no hidden fees whatsoever. The offer we make is the exact amount you receive at closing.

How fast can we actually close?

We have closed in as few as 5 days. Typically 7-14 days depending on your timeline. If you need more time — 30, 45, 60 days — that is completely fine too. You are in control.

What types of properties do you buy?

Single family homes, multi-family (2-4 units), inherited properties, vacant homes, homes in foreclosure, fire or water damaged homes, homes with foundation issues — across all 6 Midwest states we serve.

What if I owe more than the house is worth?

We can still help. We work with sellers in a variety of financial situations including underwater mortgages. Submit your address and let us talk through your options.
