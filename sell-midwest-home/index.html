Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you close on my house?
We can close in as few as 7 days once an offer is accepted. If you need more time, no problem — we work on your schedule. The key is that you set the closing date, not us.
Do I need to make repairs or clean the house?
Absolutely not. We buy houses in any condition — fire damage, foundation issues, outdated kitchens, full of stuff. You can leave whatever you don't want behind and walk away. We handle the rest.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero fees. Zero commissions. We pay all standard closing costs. The cash offer we give you is what lands in your bank account at closing. No surprises, no deductions.
Is your offer fair? How do you calculate it?
We base our offers on the property's condition, comparable sales in your area, and local market data. We're transparent about our process and happy to explain how we arrived at your number. We're not here to lowball — we want fair deals that work for everyone.
Can you help if I'm facing foreclosure?
Yes — and time is critical. We can often close before a foreclosure auction date. Reach out immediately if you're facing foreclosure. We'll assess the timeline and tell you honestly whether we can help stop it.
